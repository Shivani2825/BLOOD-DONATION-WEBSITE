Welcome to LifeStream

LifeStream is an initiative to connect, digitize and streamline the workflow of blood banks across the nation, you can get nearby camps, bloodbanks.We connect blood donor with blood seeker so that a donor can see visible impact of a donor to visit hospital or blood bank to donate blood to a patient.We provide a 360° solution to the problem: from motivating people to donate blood, to helping them donate via camps and finally helping anyone in dire need via a helpline.The organization now caters to any requirement pertaining to blood and by uniting people in need of blood to voluntary blood donors.With your support we hope to further expand and help out blood banks across the country and make them blood sufficient.
